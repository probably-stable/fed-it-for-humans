A-130 is one of those documents that's important precisely because it's invisible. It governs almost everything about federal information security policy, but most people who do federal IT work for years never read it. They read the agency policies that derive from it. Knowing A-130 exists, and that it's the source for those agency policies, is the useful piece. The text itself can wait until you have a specific reason to open it.
