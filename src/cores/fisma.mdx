A useful mental shape: FISMA is the constitutional layer. It says "thou shalt have an information security program" but doesn't tell you what that means. The "what that means" is delegated downward. By the time you're hardening a RHEL server, you're doing work that has FISMA in its ancestry but is many layers removed from the statute itself.
