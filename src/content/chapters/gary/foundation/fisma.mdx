If you sell to federal agencies, FISMA is the WHY behind your entire compliance program. Federal agencies can only buy your service if it satisfies FISMA, and FedRAMP is the standardized mechanism they use to verify that. Every agency CIO authorizing your service is making a FISMA-driven decision. You probably won't ever read the law text, but your audit calendar is shaped by it.
