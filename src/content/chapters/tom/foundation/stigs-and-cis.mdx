CIS Benchmarks are designed for you. Free, voluntary, widely adopted, intentionally accessible, Level 1 is meant to be deployable in normal IT environments without breaking anything important.

If you want a free hardening reference for your servers, CIS Benchmarks are the door. Vendor guides, Microsoft's Security Baselines, Red Hat's hardening guide, VMware's hardening guide, are the other free option, often more current for newer products.

You don't need STIGs. You can read them, they're public, but they're optimized for environments stricter than yours and they often disable functionality you'd want enabled.
