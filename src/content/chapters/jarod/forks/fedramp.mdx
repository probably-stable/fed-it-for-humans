FedRAMP-focused roles concentrate at SaaS companies that sell to federal agencies. Common job titles: FedRAMP Compliance Manager, Cloud Security Engineer (FedRAMP), GRC Analyst (FedRAMP-focused). Often hybrid private-sector / fed culture — the company is private, the compliance work is fed-shaped.

These roles tend to value: NIST 800-53 fluency (since FedRAMP is built on it), familiarity with continuous monitoring tooling, ability to write SSPs and respond to assessor findings, comfort with multi-stakeholder communication (CSP / 3PAO / agency).

**First study move:** spend 30 minutes on the FedRAMP PMO's training materials at fedramp.gov. Free, official, and gives you the program's own framing of what it is and how it works. Better than any third-party explainer.
</YouPanel>
