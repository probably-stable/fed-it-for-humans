A-130 is the second link in the chain after FISMA. You don't need to read it. You need to know it exists and that it's the OMB policy operationalizing FISMA into agency obligations.

If you interview for a federal sysadmin role, A-130 will probably not come up directly. If you interview for an agency CIO-office or compliance role, it will. Different layers of the same world.

**First study move:** read the executive summary on whitehouse.gov's A-130 landing page (skip the PDF). The summary gives you the shape and where A-130 sits in the chain in a few minutes; the Circular itself is government-policy prose you don't need to wade through.
